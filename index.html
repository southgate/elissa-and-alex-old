Getting There

Reno-Tahoe Airport (RNO)

About 1.5 hrs from the venue. Stanford Sierra Camp offers a shuttle service. We will provide more information about this as the date approaches.

San Francisco Airport (SFO)

San Francisco Bay Area (SFO/OAK/SJC). The drive from the Bay Area to Stanford Sierra Camp usually takes about 3 1/2 hours but can vary based on the weight of your right foot.
